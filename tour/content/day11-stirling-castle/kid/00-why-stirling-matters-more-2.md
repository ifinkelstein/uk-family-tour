# When Did All This Happen?
Old castles can make time feel confusing, so let me help you figure out when everything happened, using things you might already know about.

First, this is all way before phones, cars, and electricity. It is even before Christopher Columbus sailed across the ocean to America. So imagine a world with no engines at all, just horses, swords, and stone castles.

The big battles at Stirling, with William Wallace and Robert the Bruce, happened about seven hundred years ago. That is the time of knights in shining armour, riding horses and fighting with swords and shields. Think of a classic knight at a castle, and you have got the right picture.

The beautiful painted palace here was built later, about five hundred years ago. That was around the same time Columbus was sailing to America, and when famous artists like Leonardo da Vinci were painting in Italy. This is when the baby queen, Mary, was crowned here.

And the very last big battle near this castle happened about two hundred and eighty years ago, when a prince tried to grab back the throne with a Highland army.

So Stirling's story is really long. It stretches all the way from the time of knights to a time much closer to today. That is why this one castle has so many amazing stories. People have been fighting over it, and living in it, for hundreds and hundreds of years.
